## Features

- **Mark Attendance** - Quickly log your presence or absence for classes
- **View Details** - See your overall attendance statistics at a glance
- **Bunk-O-Meter** - Find out exactly how many classes you can safely skip without dropping below the minimum attendance requirement
- **Warnings** - Get alerts when your attendance is getting dangerously low
- **Reset Data** - Clear all records and start fresh for a new semester

## Why BunkBuddy?

Never stress about attendance percentage again. BunkBuddy does the math so you know exactly where you stand and how many classes you can afford to miss.

## How to Run

Run main.py 

## Usage

1. Run the program
2. Choose an option from the menu
3. Follow the prompts to track your attendance
